About Us

Building a better experience for property owners and tenants through technology and personal attention.

The Cromwell Land & Estate LLC is a property management and real estate company founded by Edward Cromwell, a U.S. veteran committed to providing quality housing and reliable property management services.

We operate primarily in Virginia, serving residential tenants with well-maintained properties and responsive management. Our business is registered in Texas and built on a foundation of integrity, efficiency, and modern technology.

What sets us apart is our technology-forward approach. We leverage AI-assisted systems for faster communication, proactive maintenance tracking, and streamlined operations — ensuring both property owners and tenants get the attention they deserve.

Privacy Policy

The Cromwell Land & Estate LLC ("we", "our") uses SMS messaging to communicate important notifications to tenants, clients, and business contacts. We collect phone numbers solely for the purpose of sending property management updates, reminders, payment confirmations, and important alerts.

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. SMS delivery is handled through Twilio, a trusted communications platform, in accordance with their privacy policy. Message data is used solely for property management operations.

Recipients may opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP to any message. For questions about this policy, contact edwardcromwell1998@gmail.com.

Terms & Conditions

SMS Messaging Program: The Cromwell Land & Estate LLC sends SMS notifications related to property management, rent reminders, maintenance updates, and important alerts. Message frequency varies based on property activity and tenant needs. Standard message and data rates may apply.

Opt-In: Recipients are added to our messaging program with explicit verbal or written consent. Tenants may be enrolled during lease signing. Other contacts are added only with direct authorization.

Opt-Out: Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe. Reply HELP for assistance. After opting out, you will receive no further messages unless you opt back in.

Supported Carriers: SMS delivery is supported on all major US carriers including AT&T, T-Mobile, Verizon, US Cellular, and others.
