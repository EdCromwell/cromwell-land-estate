What messages will I receive?

The Cromwell Land & Estate LLC sends SMS notifications related to:

  • Property management updates and announcements

  • Maintenance request updates and scheduling
  • Lease renewal reminders
  • Emergency or urgent property alerts

Message frequency varies based on property activity, typically 1–10 messages per month. Standard message and data rates may apply.
